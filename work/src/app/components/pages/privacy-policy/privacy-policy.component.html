PRIVACY POLICY

Trust LNS’s COMMITMENT TO CUSTOMER PRIVACY

At Trust LNS, we value your relationship with us, and we know that the respect for your privacy is the very foundation of that relationship. Since the inception of Trust LNS, we’ve built a reputation based on trust and confidentiality. Advances in technology have made safeguarding private information all the more important. In building customer loyalty, we promise to safeguard every information our customers share with us with utmost importance.

ABOUT THIS PRIVACY NOTICE

This notice answers some important questions about customer information. It describes our privacy policies including how we collect, use and safeguard customer information. It also explains how customers can direct us not to share certain types of information within our group of companies.

WHO IS COVERED BY THIS NOTICE?

This notice applies to all of our current and former customers.

WHAT KINDS OF INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT?

We collect the following types of information about our customers:

  • Income, marital status, age, ID proof or passport copies and other information from applications and other related forms you complete for us.
  • Transaction history, account balances and other information relating to your account with our affiliates.
  • Credit histories and other details such as demographic information we receive from our affiliates and other third parties with the title/name "Customer Information."

    • HOW DO WE USE THIS INFORMATION TO CUSTOMERS’ BENEFIT?

    We use Customer Information to provide superior service and value to our customers, in other words, we invest in Customer Loyalty. Sharing Customer Information can save time because our customers are not continually supplying our affiliates with the same information. It also allows the customers to get assistance through our telephone and digital customer service center.

    HOW DO WE PROTECT CUSTOMER INFORMATION?

    Our employees have limited access to Customer Information. They have access only when it will help customers do business with us or help us do business with our customers. We maintain physical, electronic and procedural safeguards that comply with strict internal regulations to guard Customer Information.

    DO WE SHARE INFORMATION WITH OUTSIDE COMPANIES?

    We do not sell Customer Information to outside companies. We may provide Customer Information to companies that perform marketing services for us or to financial institutions with which we have joint marketing agreements. These companies are obligated to keep Customer Information confidential. We also share Customer Information with others when it is required by law.

    DOES THE CUSTOMER HAVE A CHOICE ABOUT SHARING THIS CUSTOMER INFORMATION?

    Customers may call us at +971 4 377 9777 and someone will be able to answer questions about our privacy policy and customers may request not to share information at all. However, please note that we are permitted by law to share details and experiences such as account balances and transaction history within our family of companies.
